Senior Stylist

Cerys Davies

Senior Stylist & Colour Specialist

With over eight years behind the chair, Cerys is the studio's lead colourist. She began her career at a top Cardiff salon before bringing her expertise to Lemonade Hair, where she's helped build the studio's reputation for exceptional colour work.

Her speciality lies in creating natural, dimensional colour — from seamless lived-in balayage to precise, complex colour corrections. She approaches every client with patience, curiosity, and a standard she refuses to compromise on.

Stylist

Megan Williams

Stylist & Olaplex Specialist

Megan joined Lemonade three years ago and quickly became the go-to stylist for clients seeking healthier, stronger hair. Olaplex-certified and passionate about the science behind hair care, she brings exceptional technical depth to every appointment.

Beyond her Olaplex expertise, Megan is known for her precision cuts and ability to listen — truly understanding what each client wants and delivering it with a calm, thoughtful touch that keeps them coming back.
